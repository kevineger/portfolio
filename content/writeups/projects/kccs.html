The Kelowna Childcare Society

The Kelowna Childcare Society is a NPO whose website a team and I redesigned as a school project. Together, my team and I conducted multiple user interviews, orchestrated card-sorting activities and built the site from the ground up. The project was a collaborative team effort however I was responsible primarily for implementation of the site.

Skills practiced/utilized with this project: HTML, CSS, project management and database design.
